What is craniosacral therapy?

Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy is a non-invasive treatment that supports the health of the whole being, especially the nervous system. Craniosacral touch is profound and sets off a deep physiological reaction in the body.

How craniosacral therapy works

Performed on a treatment table, the client is fully clothed and the touch is light and still. The therapist connects with the underlying forces of health and engages the natural self-repair mechanisms of the body.

How can it help me?

Chronic pain and Fatigue

Craniosacral Therapy provides support so you can live life to your full potential, unhindered by chronic pain, low energy and recurring illnesses. Patterns of tension, strain and resistance are resolved and balance is restored.

Mothers and Babies

Craniosacral Therapy is a great support during pregnancy and aids recovery after birth. Babies respond really well to the treatment, especially if they have issues with feeding, digestion or sleeping.

Stress related illness

By bringing clients' awareness into their own bodies and surroundings, craniosacral Therapy regulates the autonomic nervous system and helps release stress. The therapy provides a deep sense of resourcing within the client, giving them a safe container for emotional issues to resolve.
